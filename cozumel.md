## ðŸ´ Authentic Local Food
Skip the port-side chains. These spots are verified active for 2026:

### 1. Mercado Municipal (The Local Market)
This is where the island eats. Walk to the back of the market to find the *loncherias* (food stalls).
* **Order:** *Cochinita Pibil* (slow-roasted pork) or *Panuchos*.
* **Location:** Corner of **Adolfo Rosado Salas** and **25th Ave**.
* **Vibe:** Bustling, local, and incredibly cheap.

### 2. Taqueria El Mexicano
A no-frills, high-quality spot famous for its *Al Pastor*.
* **Location:** **10th Ave Sur** (between Calle Salas and Calle 3).
* **Vibe:** Plastic chairs, cold beer, and the best tacos in the area.

### 3. La Choza
A local institution for sit-down regional Mexican cuisine. 
* **Order:** *Sopa de Lima* (Lime Soup) or *Pollo Pibil*.
* **Location:** **10th Ave and Calle Salas**.
